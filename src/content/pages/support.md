# Support HeatSync Labs

HeatSync Labs thrives because of community support. There are several ways you can help keep our doors open and our tools running for everyone to enjoy.

## Ways to Support Us

### Monthly Membership

The most impactful way to support HeatSync Labs is to [become a member](/membership). Members get access to storage, voting rights, and the satisfaction of directly supporting our mission.

### One-Time Donation

Make a one-time contribution through PayPal. Every dollar helps us maintain equipment, pay rent, and keep the space accessible to everyone.

### Cash or Check

Prefer to donate in person? You can bring cash or a check directly to any member at the lab during open hours. Make checks payable to "HeatSync Labs".

**Our Address:**
108 W Main St
Mesa, AZ 85201

### Shop Our eBay Store

Purchase items from our eBay store. We sell surplus equipment, member projects, and other interesting finds. Every purchase helps fund our operations.

### Equipment Donations

Have tools or equipment you no longer need? We accept donations of working tools, electronics, raw materials, and other items that benefit our community. Contact us first to ensure we can use your donation.

### Corporate Sponsorship

Interested in corporate sponsorship opportunities? We offer various sponsorship levels for businesses that want to support maker education and community development in Mesa.

Contact us to learn about sponsorship packages and benefits.

## Why Your Support Matters

### Equipment Maintenance
Keeps our 3D printers, laser cutters, and workshop tools in working condition.

### Facility Costs
Covers rent, utilities, and insurance for our 3,200 square foot facility.

### Community Access
Ensures the space remains open and accessible to everyone during public hours.

### Educational Programs
Supports classes, workshops, and educational initiatives for all skill levels.

## Thank You

HeatSync Labs exists because of supporters like you. Whether you become a member, make a donation, or simply spread the word about what we do, you're helping to build something special in Mesa. Thank you for being part of our community.
